The game has 2 main phases: night and day.

Night Phase

During the night phase, players will take any secret actions that their role gives them. Evil players will be able to talk to and plan with their teammates about what is best to do, while the Town players will have time alone with their thoughts to try to piece together what is going on and who they can trust.

Day Phase - discussion & voting

The day opens with announcements of who has perished during the night. Then, during the first half of the day phase, all living players will be able to talk together about who they suspect of being evil or who they trust as members of the Town. Simultaineously, the living players may vote to put one of them on trial for conspiracy against the town.

Day Phase - trials

Once 24 hours of discussion have passed, or a player has received more than half of the total living players' votes, the second half of the day phase starts. Whichever player has the most votes is put on trial, and may give a defense as to why they should not be killed. Afterwards, all other living players may choose to vote to condemn them, or spare them. If more people want them dead than alive, then the player is executed, and the day ends. Otherwise, the day returns to the discussion phase.

...And Repeat!

The day-night cycle continues until only 1 faction is left standing! Hopefully, it'll be the Town... right, fellow villager?
